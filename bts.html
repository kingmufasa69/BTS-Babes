    Who are BTS?

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed in Seoul in 2013. In 2018, BTS had the second best-selling album wordwide. In 2019, their album, Map of the Soul: Persona became the best-selling album in Korean history, and BTS became the best-selling artist in South Korea of all time, having sold over fourteen million albums.
